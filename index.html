For the purposes of this web page, I will discuss two important topics: How much I like museums, and how I have a totally awesome cat.

So Many Museums

I've always loved museums and archives and visit new ones whenever I can. I have been fortunate to work in some incredible ones over the past eleven years.

List of Museums

Pickles the Cat

The aforementioned great cat is named Pickles, and she's just the best. We got her as a stray six years ago in Topeka, Kansas after she'd been super sick from a neck infection. She's part ragdoll and part calico. The ragdoll portion means that when she's happy or scared she goes completely limp, which is both funny and adorable.
